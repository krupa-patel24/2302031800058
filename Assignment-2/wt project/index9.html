FAQ
1. What is this platform about?
This platform allows musicians to showcase their talent, take on challenges, and raise funds for charity through their performances.
2. How does it work?
Musicians play and share their performances, supporters donate, and funds go directly to charitable causes.
3. How do I start a challenge?
Sign up, create a challenge (like performing a song or writing music), and share it with friends and supporters.
4. Can I support without playing music?
Absolutely! You can donate, share campaigns, or challenge others to participate.
5. Is it free to join?
Yes! Creating an account and participating in challenges is completely free.
